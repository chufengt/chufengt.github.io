Biography

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Xiaolin Hu. I am also a member of TSAIL Group directed by Prof. Bo Zhang and Prof. Jun Zhu. Before that, I received my B.E. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2018.

I am a research intern at Huawei Cloud, starting from September, 2020, advised by Prof. Qi Tian and Dr. Lingxi Xie. I was a research intern from August, 2017 to July, 2018 at SenseTime, advised by Dr. Jing Shao.

My research interests primarily lie in deep learning and computer vision. Recently, I am working on instance-level detection/segmentation, language-driven visual recognition, AI for (biomedical) science, etc.

I am looking for a full-time job, please feel free to reach out to me!
