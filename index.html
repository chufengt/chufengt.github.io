Biography

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Xiaolin Hu. I received the B.E. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2018. I was a research intern from August, 2017 to July, 2018 at SenseTime, working with Dr. Jing Shao.

My research interest includes deep learning and computer vision. I was also interested in developing more effective and robust AI models based on findings in neuroscience.
